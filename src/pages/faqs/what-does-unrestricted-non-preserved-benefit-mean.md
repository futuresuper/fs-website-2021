Unrestricted non-preserved benefits are the portion of your funds that you can access, as you have already met [a condition of release](https://www.futuresuper.com.au/faqs/what-is-a-condition-of-release) to access your super. These benefits can be paid to you at any time.Â 

Typically, retired members who have chosen to leave money in their super fund will have this type of benefit.
